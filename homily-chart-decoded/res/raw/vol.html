VOL

a. Indicator Introduction

Volume is an indicator which reflects the amount of money in stock market. If the money is plentiful, the volume will increase, otherwise it will shrink. The volume is the power of the stock market to promote the price to rise. Volume average usually uses 10 days or 25 days average value as indicator.

b. Usage Principle

a) Volume moves ahead of the price. If the intraday volume is higher than 10-day average, it means the market moods are higher up; otherwise it means the market moods lower down.

b) If price rises with enlarged volume, it means that the moods boom and you may open a position.

c) The stock breaks through resistance level with enlarged volume, you may open a position.

d) The price still rises but the average volume shrinks in the epilogue of bull market. This means the buy-in will is weak and the price may fall at any time.

e) The price still drops but the average volume enlarges in the epilogue of bear market. This means the buy-in will is strong and the price may rise at any time.

f) If price falls temporarily with shrinking volume and then it rises with increasing volume in the uptrend, the price is in stabilization.
