Red & Green Signal

1.Indicator Introduction

Red & Green Signal is an indicator which is of sensitive buy point but dull sell point. This asymmetric signal is suitable for large quotation. It can filter some wrong operation in large moment.

2.Usage principle

i.Red candlestick stands for strong area, green candlestick stands for weak area.

ii. It has better effect when combining with tendency indicator (such as Homily Rainbow).
