EXPMA (Exponential Moving Average)

a. Indicator Introduction

EXPMA (Exponential Moving Average) is one kind of average line indicators. It is developed for the reason that MA is a lagging indicator, that is, to solve the slowness of MA that once the price breaks away from MA and the difference enlarged while the average line can't respond at once.

b. Usage Principle

a) When short-term EXPMA upwards breaks through long-term, it will push up the price.

b) When short-term EXPMA downwards breaks through long-term, it will weigh down the price.

c) EXPMA reacts rapidly with price fluctuation. Its usage principles are the same as that of the moving average.
