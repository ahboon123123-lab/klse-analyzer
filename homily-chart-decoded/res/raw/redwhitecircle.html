Red & White Circle

1.Indicator Introduction

SAR (Stop and Reverse) is the simplest analysis tool, which pays equal attention to time and price is a stop-loss reversal indicator. It is also called Parabola for the reason that every point of the indicator moves in arch path.

2.Usage Principle

i. Usage Principle of SAR breakthrough. When the intraday closing price downwards breaks through SAR, it’s a sell signal. When the intraday closing price upwards breaks through SAR, it’s a buy signal.

ii. When in consolidation, SAR has a higher rate of faults.

iii. When the market trend is obvious, the turning point signal is quite accurate. When we combine SAR with DMI, the effects will be better.
