Homily Rainbow

Indicator Introduction：

Homily Rainbow is a tendency indicator developed on the base of moving average combing with volume and time factors. The largest characteristic of Homily Rainbow is to trace the general direction. Homily Rainbow is made up of five color lines. The Red and Yellow line is fast speed line, it is suitable for tracing and analyzing medium and short term trend. The Yellow line is medium-speed line, it is suitable for tracing and analyzing mid and long term trend. The White Line is a slow speed line. It is suitable for tracing and analyzing long term trend. In addition, the Green Line is a medium trading auxiliary line.

Usage Principle:

i. When Homily Rainbow turns up. Price retreats to Yellow Line of Homily Rainbow and price is supported by Yellow Line. It is a medium-short term buy signal.

ii. When Homily Rainbow turns down and stock price rebounds to Yellow Line of Homily Rainbow and price is resisted by Yellow Line. It is a medium-short term sell signal.

iii. When Homily Rainbow turns up and stock price retreats to Blue Line and White Line of Homily Rainbow and price is supported by Blue Line and White Line, it is a mid-long term buy signal.

iv. When Homily Rainbow turns down and stock price rebound to Blue Line and White Line of Homily Rainbow and price is resisted by Blue Line and White Line, it's a medium-long term sell signal.

v. When the Yellow Line of Homily Rainbow concentrates and price moves in medium-term sideway, we should wait the clear trend direction and then follow trend to operate.

vi. When Yellow Line and White Line of Homily Rainbow concentrates and stock price moves in long-term sideway, it is only can operate for mid-short term.
