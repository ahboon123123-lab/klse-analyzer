Homily Position

Indication Introduction

We can predict the future trend through the integrated research of price volume relation. At the same time, Homily Position shows the strength comparison of the long & the short.

Usage Principle:

i. When life line turns down and price moves below life line, the rise should be rebound quotation. When life line turns up and price moves above the life line, the rise should be uptrend.

ii. When life line turns down and price moves below the life line, the falling should be downtrend. When life line turns up and price moves above the life line, the falling should be correction of uptrend.

iii. When Homily Position appears Increase, it is a buy signal.

iv. When Homily Position appears Decrease, it is a sell signal.

v. When price moves below the downward resonance band and price is resisted by the downward resonance band, it is a signal for holding money.

vi. When price moves above the upward resonance band and price is supported by the downward resonance band, it is a signal for holding stock.
