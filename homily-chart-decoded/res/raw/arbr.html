ARBR

a. Indicator Introduction of AR

BR cannot be used alone and it should be combined with AR, helping us identify high and low price area effectively. AR represents popularity, based on the principles of anti-market operation, when the stock market is in a fever, we had better close our position; while in pessimism, we can open a position; BR is on behalf of vigor, measuring the real potential kinetic energy of the market. It provides the trading signals according to the subtle changes of AR & BR.

Both BR and AR base on historical stock price, AR pays more attention to the opening price and reflects the trading mood of the market; While BR pays more attention to the closing price and reflects the trading wills of the market. The two indicators analyze the price volatility from different angles to track the price direction in the future.

b. Usage Principle for AR

a) The center of AR indicator value is 50. When in the vicinity of 50, the price is in consolidation and it won't rise or fall fiercely.

b) When AR reaches 80 or above, the price may turn to fall.

c) When AR falls to 20 or below, the price may turn to rise.

d) When AR is high, the market is active; when it is too high that the market comes to the top, and we had better withdraw. When AR is low, it means the market is still bear; when it is too low, it means the price in the valley floor, and we can open a position.

e) AR has the predicting function of reaching the top or bottom before price.

f) AR route shows trading mood of a certain period.

c. Indicator Introduction of BR

BR, which is also called the trading will indicator, is calculated by a fixed formula of yesterday's close price compared with intraday High and Low.

d. Usage Principle for BR

a) When the value of BR ranges from 50 to 60, the price is in consolidation;

b) When the value of BR is above 75, we should note that the price may turn into correction;

c) When the value of BR is below 20, we should note that the price may turn into rebound;

d) Generally speaking, AR can be used alone while BR should be used together with AR to exert its value;

e) The rapid rising of AR & BR means the price peak is coming;

f) If BR is lower than AR, we can open a position in a low price;

g) If BR falls from the high area and decreases by 1/2, we had better take advantage of the low price to open a position;

h) If BR rapidly rises and AR is in consolidation or correction, we had better distribute at high price.
