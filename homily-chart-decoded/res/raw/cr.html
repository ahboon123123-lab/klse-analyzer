CR

a. Indicator Introduction

CR is similar to AR and BR with the only difference in its formula. It compares the buying power with the selling power in relation to its previous mid price. There are several ways of calculating the mid price. The mid price in ShareChart is derived by the average of high and low.

b. Interpretation

The normal value range for CR is between 40 and 300. If AR goes below 40, it signals oversold. If it goes above 300, it signals overbought. If prices make a new high (low) but CR fails to make new high (low), it signals up (down) trend divergence.
