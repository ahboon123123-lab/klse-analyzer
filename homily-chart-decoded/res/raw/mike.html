MIKE (Mike Base)

a. Indicator Introduction

MIKE is a path indicator with another form. It calculates the support and resistance values of the Weak, Medium and Strong ribbons based upon typical price.

b. Usage Principle

a) There are 6 lines in MIKE indicator: 3 support lines in the lower side and 3 resistance lines in the upper side.

b) When the stock price rises to the direction of resistance lines, the lower support lines do not have reference value.

c) When the stock price falls to the direction of support lines, the upper resistance lines do not have reference value.
