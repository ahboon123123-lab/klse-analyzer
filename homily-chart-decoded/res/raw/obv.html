OBV

a. Indicator Introduction

OBV (On Balance Volume) is the main analysis tool of USA investment analyst Lee Granville. OBV is one of the most important indicators. OBV quantifies the amount of volume into trend line to coordinate with price trend line. OBV is used to judge the market sentiment from the price and volume change. It bases on the main theory that market price changes must acquire the coordination of volume and the fluctuations of stock prices is associated with the expansion or contraction of volume. Usually the rising of stock price requires larger volume while the falling of stock price requires smaller volume. If the volume doesn't correspond with the price movement, the price is hard to sustain its change.

b. Usage Principle

a) When the stock price rises while OBV falls, it means the buyers are weak and the price may turn to correction.

b) When price falls while OBV rises, it means the buyers are strong and the price may turn to rebound.

c) When OBV rises slowly, it means the force of buyers is gradually strengthened, and it is a buy signal.

d) When OBV rises rapidly, it means the force of buyers will be exhausted, and it is a sell signal.

e) OBV has a clear standard to confirm the second peak of the double tops. When the stock price rallies after the falling from the first peak, if OBV is coordinated with price trend and the volume coordinates with it, the market will keep bullish and the price will hit a new high; while OBV doesn't coordinate with it but falls to appear a second top which forms the double top pattern, the price may reverse to drop.

f) When the cumulative value of OBV line changes from positive to negative, it is a downtrend and we had better close our position; while the cumulative value of OBV line changes from negative to positive,it is an uptrend and we should open our position.

g) The greatest utility of OBV is to observe when the trend struggles away from the consolidation and predict the future trend after breakthrough. The fluctuation direction of OBV is an important reference indicator.
