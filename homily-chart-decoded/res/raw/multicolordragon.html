Multicolor Dragon (MCD)

Indicator Introduction：

Multicolor Dragon, an indicator which combines with the volume, price, time and space, is developed by the principle of transferring the strength of the long and the short. By it we can analyze the Floating Chips, Profitable Chips and Locked Chips clearly in the stock market.

Usage Principle

i. When the price rises step by step and the Profitable Chips continue to keep in a high area, Floating Chips and Locked Chips are relatively small; it means the investors hold their stocks when price rises.

ii. When the price continues to fall and the Profitable Chips reduce step by step, the Locked Chips relatively increase and Floating Chips have relatively small changes; it means the market is bearish.

iii. When Floating Chips increase in high area and Locked Chips begin to appear, it means the market enters the top area so that we should be cautious.

iv. As the falling of price, the Lock-in Chips increases and Floating Chips decreases. When Floating Chips presents heavy volume once again, it forecasts that the bottom is shaped.

v. The pink line, purple line and blue line are respectively the average lines of Floating Chips, Profitable Chips and Lock-in Chips. During designed process, the purple line of Floating Chips and Blue Line of Locked Chips are designed conversely for the convenience of investors.
