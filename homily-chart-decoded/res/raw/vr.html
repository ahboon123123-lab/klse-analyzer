VR

a. Indicator Introduction

VR (quantitative indicator) is the ratio of the total amount of ascending trading days to that of descending trading days. VR can reflect the vigor of trading and the activity of market, and master the future tendency.

b. Usage Principle

a) It is normal when the value of VR is from 80% to 150% and the price fluctuation is small at this time.

b) When VR is more than 350%, price enters the warning area and we should distribute or sell short.

c) When VR is lower than 60%, price enters the oversold area and we can catch the opportunity to open a position.

d) VR rockets with the sudden increase in amount and it will bring an ascending trend.

e) When VR rises while price doesn't, it is an opportunity to open a position.

f) When VR rises while price rockets, we should be cautious to distribute.

g) When VR increases to the value of 160% ～ 180%, volume will enter a period of recession, it is easy to come into a downtrend period after touching the top. On the contrary, when VR falls to 40% ～ 60%, it is easy to rebound after touching the bottom.

h) In general, VR is more suitable for the low price level. When in high level, we had better refer to other indicators.
